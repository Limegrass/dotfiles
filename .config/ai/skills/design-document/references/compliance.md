# Compliance & Regulatory Considerations

Apply when the design falls under regulatory, contractual, or certification scope.
Distinct from the security/privacy controls themselves -- this is the external obligation.

## Applicable Standards

- Which regimes apply (payment-card, health-data, data-protection/privacy, information-security,
  government-cloud, and similar regimes).
- Which specific controls the design must satisfy, and who confirms scope.

## Audit & Evidence

- What must be demonstrable to an auditor; logging and retention for audit trails.
- Control ownership and review cadence; evidence collection automated where possible.

## Certifications & Attestations

- Required sign-offs before launch; who approves.
- Data-processing agreements; third-party / sub-processor attestations.

## Licensing

- Open-source license obligations (copyleft, attribution, distribution constraints).
- Third-party / vendor contractual terms and usage limits.
